Discipline in the Examination Hall – Important Instructions

  1. Candidates must check the Hall Number and seating plan daily, enter the hall 15 minutes before the examination, and occupy the allotted seat correctly.
  2. Candidates must carry their Admit Card and Identity Card and ensure the answer booklet is complete, undamaged, and filled with correct details (Register No., Course, Date, etc.).
  3. On receiving the question paper, candidates must verify the Course Code/Name and immediately report any missing data or discrepancies to the invigilator.
  4. Silence must be maintained throughout the examination; candidates must sign the attendance sheet before leaving the hall.
  5. Possession of mobile phones, programmable calculators, notes, or any unauthorized material is strictly prohibited; malpractice will attract severe disciplinary action, including cancellation of exams.
  6. Candidates shall not leave the hall without permission, must not detach or take answer booklets outside, and must return any issued data books or manuals before exit.
